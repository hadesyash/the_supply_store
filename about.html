Our Story

The Supply Store is your trusted partner for premium paint solutions in Gujarat. As an authorized distributor of Simpson & Company Limited, we bring decades of industry expertise and a commitment to excellence in every interaction.

Founded with a vision to provide high-quality paints and chemicals to contractors, builders, and businesses across the region, we've built our reputation on reliability, expertise, and customer satisfaction.

Our strategic location in Changodar, Ahmedabad, allows us to efficiently serve customers throughout Gujarat, ensuring timely delivery and consistent supply of premium products.

Our Partner

Addisons Paints & Chemicals

A Division of Simpson & Company Limited

As an authorized distributor of Addisons Paints & Chemicals, we're proud to bring their legacy of quality and innovation to our customers. Addisons is one of the oldest and most respected paint companies in India, established in 1948.

Part of the AMALGAMATIONS group and a division of Simpson & Company Limited, Addisons has become a significant global participant in the paint industry. They manufacture high-performance industrial and automotive coatings from their state-of-the-art facility in Chennai, serving major clients including Tata Steel, Jindal Steel, Wheels India Limited, and many other leading industrial companies.
